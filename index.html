About Your Rehab

This app is based on the NHS Fife Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy patella dislocation programme. The exercises are arranged in 3 progressive levels.

⏱ Why consistency matters

Soft tissue healing typically takes 4–6 weeks. Doing your exercises regularly — 3 times daily — dramatically reduces the risk of re-dislocation (which is common).

📈 Progressing through levels

Move to the next level only when you have full range of movement with no pain or swelling, and you feel confident at your current level. Progress is not time-bound.

🧊 Ice & pain management

Apply an ice pack (wrapped in a damp towel — never directly on skin) for 10–15 minutes, 2–3 times daily. Simple pain relief like paracetamol or ibuprofen can help.

🚨 When to seek help

Contact NHS Fife Physiotherapy (01592 647199) if symptoms are not improving after 4–6 weeks, or if you have urgent concerns. For out-of-hours urgent problems contact NHS 24 on 111.

Disclaimer: This app is a companion tool to support adherence and is not a replacement for professional physiotherapy advice. Always follow the guidance of your NHS clinician.
